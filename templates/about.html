ParkInfo Mobile

Find over 14,000 California parks, from neighborhood sites to national wilderness, plus campgrounds and more. Get driving, transit and walking directions.

ParkInfo is published by GreenInfo Network, a nonprofit organization that supports public interest groups with maps, interactive applications and information technology.

How To Use ParkInfo

  • Explore over 15,000 parks - from small urban parks to large wilderness areas.
  • Get driving, transit and walking directions to parks.
  • Print any map view to use on your trip.
  • Share your discoveries with others.
  • Get started by using the "find parks" search tool, or by zooming into the map and clicking on any park for more information.
